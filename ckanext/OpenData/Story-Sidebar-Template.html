Information Igloo

Why

The purpose of this project is to develop an interactive tool to provide the public and council employees with statistical information that is routinely requested.

It is also intended that this will help reduce not only the number of internal enquires to the PPR section but also reduce the number of FOIâ€™s Education Services needs to respond to. It is intended that this application will also provide Directorate with year-end reports.

Benefits

There is no mechanism currently that provides the public with direct access to routine data that we hold within Education Services.

There are several benefits to the development of this application. These are:

  • Reduction in staff time required to provide routine data
  • Being able to deliver data in a more timely and planned fashion
  • Transparency in that all routine data will be in the public domain
  • Providing Education Services employees with information to support staff with Quality Reviews, School Improvement Plans, Setting Priorities, Inspections, Handbooks and work with LIGs.

The Igloo website will be launched later spring/summer 2018
