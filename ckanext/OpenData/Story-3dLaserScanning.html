3D Laser Scanning

The newly acquired Laser Scanner will provide a fresh approach to the way we capture and monitor our built estate.

From point clouds of historic buildings to failing quay walls, there will be multiple applications for the technology to improve quality, reduce duplication and transform the way that we work.

Scanned buildings can be stored for future reference, they can be developed into measured CAD drawings or data rich BIM models.

The laser scanner and its development team will contribute to the evolution of our digital transformation.
